Activities at Pacific Trails

What outdoor activities are available?

Guests can hike along scenic coastal trails, explore tide pools, and take guided tours of the surrounding area. Bird watching and photography are also popular activities.

Are there indoor activities as well?

Yes, the lodge offers board games, a library, and a lounge area for relaxation. Guests can also attend organized workshops on local wildlife and nature conservation.

Are activities included with the stay?

Most outdoor and indoor activities are included at no additional cost. Some specialized tours and classes may require a separate fee, which will be clearly indicated.
